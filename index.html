About Las Nubes

Las Nubes is a rock band hailing from Miami, Florida. Formed in 2015, the band consists of four members: lead singer and guitarist Andres Moreno, bassist and backing vocalist Juan Camacho, drummer Sebastian Moreno, and keyboardist and backing vocalist Gabriel Ruiz. Las Nubes got their start playing local shows in the Miami area, quickly gaining a dedicated following with their blend of energetic rock and dreamy, psychedelic influences. In 2017, they released their debut album, "Sombras," which garnered widespread critical acclaim and solidified their place as one of Miami's most promising young rock bands. One of the standout features of Las Nubes' music is Moreno's distinct and captivating vocal style. His soaring, emotive melodies are the perfect complement to the band's driving and energetic instrumentals. Camacho's bass playing provides a solid foundation for the band's sound, while Sebastian Moreno's drumming is both propulsive and nuanced. Ruiz's keyboard work adds a layer of texture and depth to the band's songs. In live performances, Las Nubes is known for its high-energy shows and its ability to connect with audiences. Their music has been described as "a swirling, psychedelic trip" and "a sonic feast for the senses." They often incorporate elements of visual art and performance into their live shows, making each one a unique and immersive experience. In 2019, Las Nubes released their sophomore album, "Cielo," which continued to showcase the band's growth and evolution. The album featured more complex and experimental arrangements, as well as a wider range of influences, including hints of Latin American rhythms and electronic music. Despite only being together for a few years, Las Nubes has already made a big impact on the Miami music scene and beyond. They have received numerous accolades, including being named "Best Rock Band" at the Miami Music Awards in 2018 and 2019. As Las Nubes continue to grow in both their fanbase and experimentation of different styles and variations of music, we should expect Miami’s music spectrum to evolve in the coming years. With their captivating sound and dynamic live performances, Las Nubes is sure to continue making waves in the rock world for years to come.
